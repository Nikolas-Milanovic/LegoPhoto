Welcome to My Lego Photo . Simply drag or browse a file to upload your image and then click generate to see the result. What appears is a lego photo to be made out of 1 by 1 lego plates for you to build!


The colours used in the resulting photo are all colours available on lego.com that can be purchased today. If you don’t like a colour used in the resulting lego photo then you can deselect it in the drop down.

For greater customization you can also change the scale field. The greater the scale value the fewer lego pieces are required.

Finally, once you’ve generated a lego photo, building instructions are located at the bottom of the page.
